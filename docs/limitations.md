# Limitations

- The Nodal Price Stress Score and System-Adjusted Nodal Risk Score are decision-support indicators, not causal grid diagnosis.
- The dashboard is not a forward price model.
- Industrial exposure scenarios are conditional and illustrative; they are not billing estimates.
- Topology evidence is used as reviewed context, not as electrical network-study validation.
- `zona_geografica` is not yet validated in the final product layer.
- Some robustness fields only cover barras included in sensitivity top-lists.
- Public outputs are intentionally sanitized and incomplete relative to the private product.
