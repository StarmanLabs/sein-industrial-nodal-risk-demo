## LinkedIn Launch Post

```text
I built SEIN Industrial Nodal Risk Intelligence, a decision-support dashboard for industrial due diligence on nodal electricity price-stress signals in Peru.

The system converts COES marginal-price outputs into analytical signals that help prioritize which barras deserve further review under industrial exposure, contract sensitivity and topology/context assumptions.

The public demo is intentionally controlled:
- sanitized demo data;
- selected barra-level and monthly outputs;
- public methodology summary;
- visible claim boundaries;
- no raw data or full private pipeline exposed.

The objective is not to claim causal grid diagnosis or forecast electricity bills. The objective is analytical triage: turning complex nodal price information into a structured due-diligence queue for industrial energy-risk screening.

This project combines analytics engineering, energy-economics reasoning, statistical caution, dashboard product design and closed-code publication strategy.
```
